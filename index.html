Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

The Leo Tumbler features advanced double-wall vacuum insulation technology that keeps cold beverages cold for up to 24 hours and hot drinks hot for up to 12 hours. Performance may vary based on initial temperature and environmental conditions.

We recommend hand washing your Leo Tumbler with warm soapy water to maintain the integrity of the powder coating and insulation seal. The lid components are dishwasher safe on the top rack.

The Leo Tumbler features a premium powder-coated finish that is highly resistant to scratches, chips, and fading. While it's designed for daily use and can withstand normal wear and tear, we recommend avoiding abrasive materials during cleaning.

Every Leo Tumbler comes with a 1-year manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. If you experience any issues with your tumbler, simply contact our customer service team for a replacement or refund.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your Leo Tumbler, you can return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. The product must be in new, unused condition with original packaging.
